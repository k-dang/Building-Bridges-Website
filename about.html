About us

McMasters' Building Bridges is a volunteer-driven community service club. The purpose of Building Bridges includes actively enriching the lives of seniors and strengthening connections between the McMaster and the aging community by sending volunteers to work directly with them.Our plan is to bridge the McMaster community with organizations in order to aid the older demographic. As a result, Building Bridges will work towards aiding the McMaster community with a unique perspective on a changing and diverse population.

Get Involved

Like what you hear? Then come out and join us in our aim to bridge McMaster. Sign up for our email list if you haven't already and come out to our general meetings. Meet and mingle with other members who are also involved in our cause at our monthly events. Be on the look out for upcoming volunteering events as well.

Who we work with

Currently we are working with VON (Victorian Order of Nurses) to send volunteers out to get directly involved with the aging community. VON is a specialized service for adults and the programs that they host offer clients companionship and specialized services that enhance the lives of seniors. They have lots of different programs going on like Meals on Wheels for which they are looking for volunteers. There is also the Adult Day center volunteering.
